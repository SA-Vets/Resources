4. General Tips for Maximizing Claim Success

  • Start Early: Begin the claims process while still on active duty or as soon as possible after discharge. It can be beneficial to gather medical records, service treatment records, and any documentation related to injuries or health conditions.
  • Documentation is Key: Provide thorough and detailed documentation of your medical conditions, treatment history, and service-related incidents. Obtain supporting statements from medical professionals and witnesses if possible.
  • Attend VA Exams: Disability evaluations by VA doctors are essential for your claim. Be punctual, provide accurate information, and ask questions if you don’t understand something.
  • Follow Up on Your Claim: Check the status of your claim regularly through eBenefits or by contacting your representative. Respond promptly to any requests for additional information.
  • Consider Appeals if Necessary: If your claim is denied or you receive a lower rating than expected, don’t give up. Appeal decisions can lead to higher benefits, especially with the support of a VSO or legal representative.
